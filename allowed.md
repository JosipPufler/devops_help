Exam preparation
Dear students,
 
This message is to ensure that you can prepare and demonstrate your knowledge from the Intro to DevOps course at the exam time. Here is an overview of what to expect.
 
Practice questions are available in a file called "Exam practice questions" on Moodle. As you can see, learning outcome 6 is purely theoretical and will cover a broader set of topics, including an overview of Docker Swarm and k3s.
 
The exam is fully written on a VM deployed in Algebra cloud. To access Algebra's cloud, you need to configure a VPN on your PC. All the instructions for that are in a document called "Environment access instructions" available on Moodle. During the exam you open the console to your VM on Algebra cloud and from that point on, everything should be done within that VM, including browsing the web, accessing questions, writing answers and submitting them. You will also need to do screenshots inside that VM by navigating to Applications > Utilities > Screenshot.
